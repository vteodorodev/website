Vasco Duarte

About Me

Who are you?

My name is Vasco Duarte and I'm a 35 year old mobile and web frontend developer from Lisbon, specializing in React Native and ReactJS.

What did you study?

I have an MSc in Biomedical Engineering through Instituto Superior Técnico (IST) with a focus in Data Science and Signal Processing.

I'm also currently finishing an iOS Mobile Development certification at Coursera, developed by Meta.

Why mobile development?

Before starting my wonderful journey in software development, I was a researcher at IST with a goal of developing tools to help assess and monitor people with clinical depression. At some point, I was challenged by my supervisors to develop a mobile application that patients could log into to answer a simple set of surveys that could later be checked by a medical professional. Written using Flutter, this was the first mobile application I ever made, but I later developed additional applications using React Native and Android.

This challenge placed me outside my comfort zone, because despite having a solid background in coding, this was my first time developing software that went through the entire pipeline of prototyping, designing and integrating both back and frontend infrastructures.

Any other interests?

Within the realm of technology, I enjoy tinkering with small electronics projects involving both Arduino and Raspberry Pi.

In my free time, I also enjoy reading, working out and gardening. Moreover, I love learning new languages by myself, having successfully picked up Swedish in the past and I am contemplating learning Japanese in the near future.
